About the Artist


Hello! I am a third year student at
Pace University located in Manhattan, New York.
I study Psychology and Fine Arts and I
hope to become an art therapist in the future.
Growing up in the Philadelphia area, I was constantly surrounded by art and culture.
I was fortunate enough to have such great mentors
from Upper Darby High School to inspire me
to pursue art not only as a hobby, but a career.
This is a portfolio of my artworks I have created
so far during my college career. Enjoy!
